Trial Locator was founded with a singular mission: to make finding and enrolling in life-changing clinical trials as easy as possible. Medicine, particularly oncology, is undergoing a revolution. Once untreatable diseases are becoming treatable, and in some cases curable, but for many people the most promising treatments for their condition are unavailable to them because those treatments are still going through clinical trials.

The drug approval process is well intentioned and run by people who care about patient safety and wellbeing. Unfortunately, for many people the process takes too long and they die or become too sick for treatment before the drugs that could help them become available. By making it easier for people to find what trials they are eligible for and discover which of those trials are the most promising, and by reducing the friction involved in enrolling in trials, we can help more people live longer, healthier lives than they otherwise would.

We are run as a nonprofit and are in the process of converting from a C corp to a 501(c)(3) nonprofit. Our financial data can be found here. If you would like to help us serve more people, more effectively you can donate or learn how to volunteer.
